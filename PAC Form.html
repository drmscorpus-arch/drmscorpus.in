Risk Stratification Scores

RCRI — Revised Cardiac Risk Index Cardiac MACE risk in non-cardiac surgery · Use for all patients undergoing intermediate/high-risk surgery
High-risk surgery (intrathoracic / intraperitoneal / suprainguinal vascular)+1
History of ischaemic heart disease+1
History of congestive heart failure+1
History of cerebrovascular disease (TIA / stroke)+1
Diabetes mellitus on insulin+1
Pre-op serum creatinine >2.0 mg/dL+1
Score 0 → <1% MACE  ·  1–2 → 1–7%  ·  ≥3 → >11%
≥3: Consider cardiology referral before elective surgery per ACC/AHA 2014 guidelines
Functional Capacity (METs) Exercise tolerance assessment · Use alongside RCRI; <4 METs + RCRI ≥1 triggers further workup
<4 METs → Poor functional capacity  ·  ≥4 METs → Adequate
<4 METs + RCRI ≥1: further workup per ACC/AHA algorithm. Offer cardiopulmonary exercise testing if available.
ASA Physical Status Classification Overall systemic disease severity · Mandatory for all patients undergoing anaesthesia
Add "E" suffix for emergency surgery (e.g., ASA III E)
ASA III–IV: increased peri-operative morbidity; informed consent must document risk stratification
STOP-BANG Questionnaire Obstructive Sleep Apnoea (OSA) screening · Use for all obese, snoring, or hypertensive patients
Snoring — loud enough to be heard through closed doors+1
Tired — often fatigued / sleepy during daytime+1

Gender — Male+1

Caprini Score VTE / DVT thromboprophylaxis risk stratification · Use for all surgical patients
1 pt each: Age 41–60 · Minor surgery · BMI >25 · Swollen legs · Varicose veins · Pregnancy/postpartum · OCP/HRT · Sepsis <1 month · Lung disease · Bed rest · IBD1×
2 pts each: Age 61–74 · Major surgery · Malignancy · Laparoscopy >45min · Central venous access · Immobilising cast · Bed rest >72h2×
3 pts each: Age ≥75 · Prior DVT/PE · Family h/o thrombosis · Thrombophilia · Lupus anticoag / anticardiolipin Ab3×
5 pts each: Stroke <1m · Elective arthroplasty · Hip/pelvis/leg fracture · Spinal cord injury <1m · Multiple trauma <1m5×
0–1 → Very Low  ·  2 → Low (TEDS)  ·  3–4 → Moderate (LMWH or IPC)  ·  ≥5 → High (LMWH + IPC)
≥5: mechanical + pharmacological prophylaxis required
